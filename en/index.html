In the midst of nature of the Rügen Bodden landscape, you will find relaxation away from mass tourism.

Our house is located on the edge of the harbor village with a view of the Breetzer Bodden and the marina.

A fireplace and underfloor heating provide a cozy atmosphere on cold days.

On the upper floor, in addition to the bathroom and sauna, there are 2 bedrooms with box spring beds (one double bed and 2 single beds).

The open kitchen is located in the living area on the lower floor. It is fully equipped and offers all the amenities you are used to from home (ceramic hob/oven, refrigerator with freezer compartment, kettle, toaster, microwave, Jura coffee machine, milk frother, dishwasher, raclette...)

Also on the ground floor is a second bathroom with shower, toilet and the washing machine.

On the terrace there is a beach chair. Additional terrace furniture / cushions as well as an electric grill can be found in the outbuilding. For families with small children there is a baby bed and a high chair.

Our house is a non-smoking house. Animals are not allowed. This makes it also suitable for allergy sufferers.

Fast and of course free WiFi is also available.

Next to the holiday house there is a free parking space for 1 car.
