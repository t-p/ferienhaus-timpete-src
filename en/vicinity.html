Our harbor village naturally doesn't have a large supermarket, you'll find that in Gingst or Bergen. But there is nature and tranquility.

Bring your own boat, as the fully equipped marina with protected boat berths and a gas station is the ideal starting point for Baltic Sea tours. You can also rent a license-free motorboat for a fishing trip or a Bodden tour.

In the main season, a passenger ferry runs to Hiddensee. Or you can drive in 24 minutes to the ferry to Hiddensee.

The shallow shores of the Bodden invite swimming, fishing and surfing.
About 6km away there is a riding stable.
